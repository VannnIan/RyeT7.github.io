Build Master Register

BuildMatch is a professional networking platform providing a way for organizations, businesses, and individuals to connect with those of professions related to engineering and structure building. BuildMatch facilitates employers to connect with contractors and engineers with specific job types in accordance to their needs, while simultaneously providing those with the trade skills with job opportunities. Users are provided with 24/7 customer service and are provided with the choice of accessing human help or help powered by a chatbot.

BuildMatch values integrity and work safety, and hence, users who are classified as those with hands-on experience in the structure-making business and trade skills are required to submit direct proof of necessary certifications and/or experience, however, this is not limited to them, as employers are also required to provide proof of their legitimacy.
